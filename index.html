Official XContest Rules

Free Flight: Non-triangle path. 1.00 pt/km.

Free Triangle: Gap < 20% of perimeter. 1.20 pts/km.

FAI Triangle: Free triangle, shortest leg ≥ 28% of perimeter. 1.40 pts/km.

Closed Free Tri: Free triangle, gap < 5% of perimeter. 1.40 pts/km.

Closed FAI Tri: FAI triangle, gap < 5% of perimeter. 1.60 pts/km.

Wedges: Green zones where your 3rd turnpoint makes the triangle FAI-compliant.
